Issue Request Flow — MEW Reports a Problem

When MEW discovers a fault (e.g. broken sensor, leaking valve), the request goes through this approval cycle before invoicing.

📩
Step 1 · MEW
Creates Issue Request
Selects site, department, urgency, and describes the problem
👔
Step 2 · Belfinger Manager
Reviews & Assigns
Sets priority, selects supervisor, sets SLA deadline
👷
Step 3 · Supervisor
Assigns to Engineer
Picks the right site engineer based on location & skills
🔧
Step 4 · Site Engineer
Does the Work
Opens on iPad, fills checklist, takes photos, gets signatures

After Work is Completed — Invoice & Approval

Once the site engineer submits the completed work, the invoice cycle begins.

✅
Step 5 · Site Engineer
Submits Completed Work
Work report, checklist, photos & dual signatures uploaded
📋
Step 6 · Supervisor
Reviews & Creates BOQ Invoice
Checks work quality, selects BOQ items, calculates cost
✅
Step 7 · Belfinger Manager
Approves Invoice
Final internal review, approves and sends to MEW
📤
Step 8 · MEW
Final Approval
MEW reviews invoice & attached work report, approves payment
💰 Invoice becomes Receivable — Payment processed

Rejection & Rework — "Send Back To"

At any approval step, the reviewer can reject and send the work back. The system shows who you can send back to:

👔
Manager Rejects Invoice
Can send back to:
← Supervisor
"Fix BOQ line items"
👷
Supervisor Rejects Work
Can send back to:
← Site Engineer
"Need more photos"
📩
MEW Rejects Invoice
Can send back to:
← Belfinger Manager
"Missing work permit"

Preventive Maintenance (PM) Flow

PM jobs are auto-generated by the system on schedule. They skip MEW request — the cycle starts at the Supervisor.

⏰
Auto
System Creates PM
Nightly cron generates work orders per schedule
👷
Supervisor
Assigns Engineer
Routes to available engineer for that site
🔧
Site Engineer
Does PM Work
Checklist, calibration, photos, signatures
📋
Supervisor
Creates Invoice
BOQ items for completed PM work
✅
Manager
Approves
Final sign-off and send to MEW
📤
MEW
Approves
Confirms PM was completed
