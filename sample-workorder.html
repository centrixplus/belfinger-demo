Work Report (by Engineer)
"Arrived at Outlet Line 34. Inspected flow transmitter FIT-34 (Electromagnetic type). Found corroded cable gland causing intermittent signal loss. Cable insulation was damaged due to moisture ingress. Replaced cable gland and re-terminated the signal cable. Performed loop test — transmitter now reading correctly at 67.3% flow rate (confirmed against DCS reading). Calibrated to 0-100% range using HART communicator. All readings within ±0.5% accuracy."
